About Me

Sunset Cliffs @San Diego, 2021

Outside my research, I am always trying to do something filling my soul with thoughts. I would categorize myself as a nostalgic person, but I am also expecting new. My lifestyle is heavily influenced by ancient Chinese philosopher Lao Tzu. My spirit is refreshed every time reading Tao Te Ching. In my life, I have a wide range of interests. And I believe they are bridging my mind to the material world.

I claim myself nostalgic because I am enchanted by old rocks back to 60s and 70s, like the Beatles, Eric Clapton, the Rolling Stones, etc. I have visited some of those old men's concerts in Detroit and Seattle. You can find a photo of Paul and me singing Hey Jude together :-)

Paul McCartney and me @Seattle, 2022

I also love vinyl records more than digital music. Those analog medium makes me feel closer to artists. As an electrical engineer, I definitely know modern digital music has better resolution. However, listening to the full track of vinyl records is another lifestyle :-)
