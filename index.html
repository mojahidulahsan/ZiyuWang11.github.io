Research Briefing

I am working with Prof. Wei Lu in the Department of EECS, the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor. Our group is focusing on RRAM-based in-memory computing (IMC) architectures for AI acceleration.
Unlike most of researchers in this area, dedicated to improve perfomance, I am rethinking the architecture and circuit design from a security perspective. My research interests include vulnerability and security analysis of non-volatile memory based analog IMC system, as well as designing reliable and secure IMC system.
